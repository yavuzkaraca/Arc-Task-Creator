Study Information

1. Purpose:

In this study, we investigate how the use of language affects engagement. By participating, you will get an insight into our research, and you will further help us to gain new insights into human cognition.

2. Confidentiality:

We save the data under a randomly assigned participant number and thus totally anonymized on a server of TÃ¼bingen University. All people that have access to your personal data are obliged to maintain data secrecy. If data from this study is published, this will happen in an anonymized manner. Hence, your data cannot be assigned to you.

3. Voluntary participation and right to withdraw:

Your participation in this research is entirely voluntary, you can withdraw at any time. There will be no negative repercussions. You do not need to provide a reason for your withdrawal. If you withdraw from the study before data collection is completed, your data will not be used.

4. Risks:

Your participation does not involve any physical or emotional risk to you. You are free to withdraw at any time.

5. Data storage and sharing:

We will share this data with other researchers. If the study does not result in a scientific publication, we will store the data on a password-protected university server for a maximum of 5 years. Since the data is stored completely anonymized, we cannot delete your data once it has been saved.
